Portfolio Management Challenge 2025 - ENSA Agadir

📌 Project Overview
This repository contains the technical models, analysis, and final report developed for the Portfolio Management Challenge 2025, organized during the 5th Edition of the Financial Day at ENSA Agadir. 

Working as a simulated asset management firm, our team was tasked with analyzing the macroeconomic environment (inflation, interest rates, geopolitics), constructing a balanced multi-asset portfolio, and optimizing our asset allocation using quantitative finance methodologies. The simulation ran for a one-month trading period from October 25, 2025, to November 25, 2025.

## 🌍 Investment Universe & Data Sourcing
Our portfolio was diversified across traditional and modern asset classes:
**Equities:** International stocks traded on the NASDAQ.
**Cryptocurrencies:** Major digital assets like Bitcoin (BTC) and Ethereum (ETH) utilized for broader risk diversification.
**Data Sources:** Daily historical and real-time market data sourced from public platforms including Yahoo Finance.

## ⚖️ Portfolio Constraints & Risk Management
To replicate a highly regulated institutional environment, our asset allocation strictly adhered to the following competition guidelines:
* Maximum weight of 30% for any single asset line.
* Maximum global portfolio exposure to cryptocurrencies capped at 20%.
* Strict prohibition of margin trading, financial leverage, and short-selling.

## 🛠️ Methodology & Tech Stack
**Macroeconomic Analysis:** Evaluated global indicators such as inflation, interest rates, and geopolitical events to guide our defensive/aggressive posturing.
**Quantitative Optimization:** Applied risk modeling and performance analysis to maximize our risk-adjusted returns.
**Tools Used:** Python and Excel for statistical analysis, financial modeling, and calculating portfolio variance.
